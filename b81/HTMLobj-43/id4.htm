===============================
first orgasm : it's all about the attitude
===============================

brigada ocho was born with me, PetiK and ZirkoV as its original members two months ago and promised the "scene" the first b8 e-zine this month... PetiK left the virus scene, ZirkoV was super inactive and new members weren't giving some outputs so i decided to halt the existence of brigada ocho... so the b8 e-zine was also put into bin for a moment...

september 05, 2002, with a few interested vxers by my side, brigada ocho rose from its ashes and the dream of releasing the first e-zine came true this time...

i decided to revive brigada ocho again to fullfill a promise to the few who gave some support to the group by submitting their works for the proposed brigada ocho zine and to have a place for, preferably, hll coders and virus collectors out there to gather and spend some worthwhile time...

this first blast goes to PetiK and to the few supporters of brigada ocho..

enjoy reading..
